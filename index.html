About me

Hello! I'm Neha Bhatt, a passionate student pursuing a BTech in Computer Science at Graphic Era Hill University. My educational journey began at Evergreen Senior Secondary School, where I laid the foundation for my love of technology and problem-solving. My journey into the world of web development has been an exciting one. I've immersed myself in the art of creating beautiful websites, and my toolkit includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I believe that the web is a canvas for endless creativity, and I'm enthusiastic about designing and developing engaging online experiences. As a student of computer science, I'm continuously exploring new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in web development. My goal is not just to create functional websites but to craft immersive and user-friendly digital spaces that leave a lasting impression.
Apart from web development, I have a deep-rooted love for competitive programming. It's not just a hobby; it's a way for me to challenge and strengthen my logical thinking and problem-solving skills. My goal is to bridge the gap between creativity and technology, using my programming skills to bring innovative web solutions to life. I believe that the web is a canvas where art and logic converge, and I'm dedicated to creating the perfect blend of both.
Join me on this journey of innovation and creativity as we explore the limitless possibilities of web development together.
